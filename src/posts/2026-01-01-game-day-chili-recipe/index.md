## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 pounds ground chuck (I typically use 2.5 lbs)
- [ ] 1 medium onion, chopped
- [ ] 3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans, rinsed and drained
- [ ] 3 (8 ounce) cans tomato sauce
- [ ] 1 (12 ounce) bottle dark beer
- [ ] 1 (14 1/2 ounce) can beef broth
- [ ] 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- [ ] 1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chiles
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chili powder
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- [ ] 1 to 2 teaspoons ground red pepper (I typically use 3-4)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon paprika
- [ ] 1 teaspoon hot sauce (I typically use double or more)

---

## Preparation

1. Cook first 3 ingredients in a Dutch oven over medium heat, stirring until meat crumbles and is no longer pink. Drain well.
2. Combine meat mixture, beans, and next 11 ingredients in Dutch oven
3. Bring to a boil. I will boil for 15-20 minutes stirring every minute or so to avoid it burning.  I do this to help remove some of the beer taste.  
4. Reduce heat, and simmer 3 hours or until thickened.
